About This Episode

What if the universe is constantly communicating with you… but you’re not noticing?

In this episode of The Attitude of Gratitude, host Sadhna Rishi sits down with Kanikka Batra, angel card reader, spiritual coach, and founder of Hamsa Path.
This conversation explores a fascinating idea:
👉 The universe is always sending signs — you just need to be aware enough to receive them.
From angel numbers to intuitive nudges, Kanikka breaks down how spiritual communication works and how individuals can align with their higher purpose.
Her journey from a 12-year corporate career into spirituality is a powerful reminder that transformation often begins with subtle signs we choose to follow.
This episode is not about blind belief —
it’s about awareness, intuition, and inner alignment.
She opens up about:

  • What angel numbers really mean
  • How to recognize signs from the universe
  • Why self-awareness is the foundation of spiritual growth

What You'll Take Away

  • What angel numbers (111, 333, etc.) actually represent
  • The difference between intuition, signs, and guidance
  • How to “empty your mind” to receive clarity
  • Why repeating life patterns are lessons, not coincidences
  • The difference between affirmations and manifestation
  • How gratitude raises your emotional and mental frequency
  • Why journaling strengthens intuition and awareness

Featured Guest

Kanikka Batra

Founder, Hamsa Path | Angel Card Reader & Spiritual Coach

Kanikka Batra is a spiritual coach, angel card reader, and founder of Hamsa Path, a platform dedicated to helping individuals reconnect with their authentic selves.

After a 12-year corporate career, she transitioned into spirituality, guided by intuitive signs and inner alignment.

Her work focuses on helping people understand their patterns, trust their intuition, and navigate life with clarity and purpose.
