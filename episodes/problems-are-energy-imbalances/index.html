About This Episode

What if your struggles are signals… not problems?

In this insightful episode of The Attitude of Gratitude, host Sadhna Rishi sits down with Disha Odhrani, energy healer and transformation coach, to explore how emotional patterns, physical issues, and life challenges are often rooted in energy imbalances.
This conversation bridges science and spirituality — helping you understand how your inner state directly influences your outer reality.
Disha explains how blocked energy, suppressed emotions, and unconscious patterns can manifest as stress, confusion, or even physical discomfort.
This is not about quick fixes —
it’s about awareness, alignment, and inner work.
She opens up about:

  • How chakras influence different areas of life
  • Why emotional blockages create real-life challenges
  • How awareness can restore balance and clarity

What You'll Take Away

  • What chakras are and how they affect your life
  • How emotional energy gets stored in the body
  • Why repeating life problems are not coincidences
  • How to identify and release energy blockages
  • The connection between thoughts, emotions, and physical health
  • Simple practices to restore balance and clarity
