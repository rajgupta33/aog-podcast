About This Episode

What does it really take to build something from nothing?

In this inspiring episode of The Attitude of Gratitude, host Sadhna Rishi sits down with Karthik Vijayamani — founder of Dubai’s Talk Show — to uncover the raw, unfiltered journey of building success from the ground up.
From sleeping on the floor during his early days in Dubai to creating a platform that gives others a voice, Karthik’s journey is a powerful reminder that success is built through consistency, belief, and action.
This is not a story of overnight success —
it’s a story of persistence through uncertainty.
He shares the realities behind entrepreneurship — the struggles, the mindset shifts, and the moments that define a founder’s journey.
He opens up about:

  • Starting from zero in a new country
  • Building a platform without resources
  • The mindset required to keep going despite failures

What You'll Take Away

  • Why starting small is better than waiting for perfection
  • The importance of resilience in entrepreneurship
  • How to stay consistent when results are slow
  • Why visibility and storytelling matter in today’s world
  • How to build something meaningful without external validation
  • The mindset shift required to grow in a competitive environment
