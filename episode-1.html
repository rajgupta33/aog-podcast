About This Episode

What does it really take to walk away from a stable corporate career and follow your passion?

In this episode, we sit down with Ruchi Poddar, who transitioned from working with companies like Infosys to building her own business after motherhood.

This is not just a story about entrepreneurship — it’s about identity, courage, and starting again.

She opens up about:

  • Leaving comfort behind
  • Facing self-doubt
  • Rebuilding herself from within

What You'll Take Away

  • Why passion often feels harder than a stable job
  • The emotional side of career transitions
  • How motherhood can reshape your identity
  • A practical mindset shift for big life decisions
  • Why gratitude is a real growth tool (not just a buzzword)

Featured Guest

Ruchi Poddar

Founder, MelonMe | Former Software Engineer

Ruchi spent years in corporate and real estate before realizing something was missing.

After motherhood, she chose to follow her passion and built MelonMe — a brand redefining celebrations with healthy, fruit-based cakes.

Her story proves:
👉 The “safe” path isn’t always the fulfilling one.
